Emergency Notification System

If you live, work or go to school in california and are 18 and over, you may now register your cell phone or VoIP (voice over internet protocol) phone to receive emergency alerts sent by call, text, or email

Be Sure You are Registered to Get the Emergency Call, Text & Email

Emergency officials use the Cal-Notify Emergency Notification System to deliver incident-specific information or potentially life-saving instruction to the precise geographic area(s) affected. Emergency situations may include:

  • flooding, wildfires, and subsequent evacuations
  • public safety incidents including crimes that affect your neighborhood
  • and post-disaster information about shelters, transportation, or supplies

Types of Alerts

Cal-Notify may inform you of emergency and inportant non-emergency events occouring in your area including:

Emergency

Alerts issued when an imminent threat to life or property exists in your area, including severe man-made or natural disasters such as earthquakes, wild fires, flooding, hurricanes, and tornadoes.

Non-Emergency

Alerts issued when events may impact citizens including, road cloasures, weather advisories, AMBER alerts,

Official

Alerts issued by the President or a designee during a national emergency.
