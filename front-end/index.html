Cal-Notify

Cal-Notify provides real time updates on emergencies and other events in your area, using official sources that you can trust.

Emergency Notification System

If you live, work or go to school in california and are 18 and over, you may now register to receive emergency alerts sent by text or email.

Be Sure You are Registered to Get the Emergency Call, Text & Email

Emergency officials use the Cal-Notify Emergency Notification System to deliver incident-specific information or potentially life-saving instruction to the precise geographic area(s) affected. Emergency situations may include:

  • Flooding, wildfires, and subsequent evacuations.
  • Public safety incidents including crimes that affect your neighborhood.
  • Post-disaster information about shelters, transportation, or supplies.

Types of Alerts

Cal-Notify may inform you of emergency and important non-emergency events occurring in your area including:

Emergency

Alerts issued when an imminent threat to life or property exists in your area, including severe man-made or natural disasters such as earthquakes, wildfires, flooding, hurricanes, and tornadoes.

Non-Emergency

Alerts issued when events may impact citizens including, road closures, weather advisories, AMBER alerts.
